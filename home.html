Is Love Quantum Mechanics?

Love exist in different ways and forms
It could be in a wavelike state, spreading out like a storm
But sometimes it looks like a particle, it's small yet filled with constant action
But let it exist in all state; chaotic, undefined, and full of reaction

Like electrons in the state of superposition
Electrons exist in different states and different configuration
Like love with undefined characterization
Its only meaning can be found through observation

Yet love's definition have a limit in its precision
We can't exactly lessen it through the amount of hesitation
and we can't exactly heighten it through presentation
But we should know that it is felt through realization

So to my TinTin who will be reading this composition
I hope you know, that our distance wouldn't cause decomposition
I hope you know, that even if your words would cause irritation
It wouldn't lead to my love's termination

Because my love for you is already defined by observation
It might not take one shape, state, and form existing like a superposition
And sometimes my emotions may vary, like pack of energy in quantization
But please always know, that me loving you will always be the result of our collapse wave-function

Thank you for being with me, my favorite Paradox
